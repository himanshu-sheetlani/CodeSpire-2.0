About The Event

Codespire is the first edition of the National Level Event to be organized under the aegis of Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research, Indore by the Google Developer Student Clubs, AITR in collaboration with the Association for Computing Machinery, AITR and Association for Computing Machinery Women, AITR

Codespire is the perfect platform for young innovative minds to showcase their potential talent of ideating to provide viable industry-ready solutions to real-world problems with a technical edge and the art of weaving creative solutions to some brainstorming problems of competitive programming through logical problem-solving skills. Our basic aim is to develop a platform for aspiring tech enthusiasts to showcase their ideas and coding skills.

F.A.Q

  • Can I participate in both coding and project contests?

    Yes, interested students are welcome to participate in both contests and explore.

  • I am not familiar with coding, can I still participate?

    No worries, willingness to learn is all that is needed to participate, as learning and growing go hand in hand.

  • Will there be any team size for the contests?

    Yes! In Project Contest the team size for participation will be 2 to 4 members.

  • Will there be any registration fees?

    Yes! The registration fees will be there.
    For Coding Contest:-
    The registration fee will be 20/- and The entry fee taken after round 1 results will be 100/- (Refundable)
    For Project Contest:-
    The entry fee taken after PPT Shortlisting will be 300/- (Refundable)

  • Are there any prizes for the winners?

    Of course, there are!! There is an exciting prize pool of 50k and many other perks including a certificate for participation and internship opportunities .
