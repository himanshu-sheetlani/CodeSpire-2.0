About The Event

CodeSpire 2.0 is a national-level coding competition designed to promote innovation, enhance logical thinking, and foster collaboration among students from colleges across the country. The event provides participants an opportunity to work on real-world problems, sharpen their programming skills, and experience the pressure of working within tight deadlines.

The competition is structured in two main rounds: an online preliminary round and an offline final round. The online round will be hosted on Unstop, ensuring a fair and accessible platform for all participants, while the offline final round will take place at AITR Indore, bringing together the best teams to compete in a high-energy, on-campus coding challenge..

F.A.Q

  • What is CodeSpire 2.0?

    CodeSpire 2.0 is a coding hackathon consisting of two rounds:
    An online round with three DSA problems
    And an offline round at Acropolis Institute of Technology and Research, Indore.

  • I am not familiar with coding, can I still participate?

    No worries, willingness to learn is all that is needed to participate, as learning and growing go hand in hand.

  • What is the prize pool for CodeSpire 2.0?

    The total prize pool for the event is 20K.

  • Will there be any registration fees?

    Yes! The registration fees will be there of 100Rs.

  • Are there any prizes for the winners?

    Of course, there are!! There is an exciting prize pool of 20k and many other perks including a certificate for participation and internship opportunities .

  • How is the winner determined in the offline round?

    The team with the maximum correct solutions in the offline round will be declared the winner.

  • What types of problems can participants expect?

    Participants can expect easy, medium, and hard-level DSA problems in both rounds.
